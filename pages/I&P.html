INNOVATION & POLICIES:


Innovations with potential for commercialization

  • Developed a ‘Point-of-Care Antibody Assay’ prototype for Lymphatic Filariasis and the patent is under preparation for filing.
  • Developed a Nano-technology based biosensor to detect filarial infection in vectors, and a patent is under preparation for filing.
  • Identified a plant extract against sand flies (vectors of Leishmaniasis) in collaboration with RMRI Patna, and the patent is under preparation.
  • Isolated an Anti-HSV molecule from a folklore with its Molecular Mechanism of Action, which is under technology transfer pipeline.
  • An Anti-arthritic formulation is under final stage of validation and patent filling.
