VISION


• To influence public health outcomes through integration with Traditional Medicine (TM).

MISSION


Research:

• To develop evidence based, affordable TM solutions for Public Health concerns.

Development and Resources:

• Create a cadre of integrative health care researchers and professionals.
• Develop programs & content for application of validated traditional knowledge.

Outreach:

• To deliver scalable and sustainable technology & practices in the community through partnerships with NGO’s, Government Organizations & private sector.
• To facilitate policy development through research and promotion.

Domain of Primary Activity:

> Multi- and Trans- Disciplinary studies in TM.
> Validation of selected TM using Modern tools & technologies.
> Collaborations with Practitioners of TM AYUSH and other stakeholders.
> Land to Lab/Clinic and Clinic to Land approach.
> Investigations on important diseases including tribal health.
